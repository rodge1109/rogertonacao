# Project Custom Rules & Preferences

## KIARAS AI Poster Design & Layout Guidelines
When generating commercial ad posters for Kiara's Home Made Ice Cream, follow these daily theme layout structures:

### General Rules Across All Posters:
1. **Small Logo at Bottom Right**: Place the official Kiara's Home Made Ice Cream logo (`kiara's ®` pink curved banner + `HOME MADE ICE CREAM` blue ribbon) small, subtle, and clean in the **bottom right corner**.
2. **Compact Contact Footer (10px)**: A sleek short 10px height dark footer bar at the very bottom edge containing contact details and CTA (`Order Yours Today! Call/Text: 09452134174 | Bogo City`).
3. **No Literal CTA Labels**: Do NOT write any literal "Call To Action" or "CTA" text box labels on the image.
