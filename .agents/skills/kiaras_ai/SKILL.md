# KIARAS AI Skill

This skill automates the daily marketing workflow for **Kiara's Home Made Ice Cream** in Bogo City, Cebu:
1. Scrapes PAGASA weather forecast for Bogo City & Cebu.
2. Extracts customer reviews & social proof.
3. Generates high-converting commercial ad posters featuring the official brand logo.
4. Formats ready-to-post Facebook campaign copy with CTAs and contact details.

- "kiara advertisement poster"
